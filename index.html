Formula Reference & Safeguards

  • Match numerator and denominator by person, place, and time.
  • Distinguish existing cases from new cases, risk from rate, odds ratio from relative risk, and hazard ratio from risk ratio.
  • Never enter identifiable patient information.
  • Adjusted rates depend on the chosen standard population.
